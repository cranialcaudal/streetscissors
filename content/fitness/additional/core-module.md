> [!tip] How to run it
> 4 min warm-up → 18 min circuit (3 rounds, **40s work / 20s rest**) across the middle three modules. Pick a kettlebell you can control through full reps — moderate, not max.

**Focus:** Building an iron chassis to transfer power safely from your legs to your throwing arm, keeping your lower back safe during the 112 miles of the Ironman bike ride, and developing a square waist with a V-taper.

This 15-minute core block is designed to be dropped onto the end of your **Tuesday** or **Thursday** lifting sessions, or done quickly at home. It trains the core in three distinct, critical ways: resisting rotation, resisting extension, and generating explosive power.
