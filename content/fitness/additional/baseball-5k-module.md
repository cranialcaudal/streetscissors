**Focus:** Passive baseball sharpening and extra aerobic volume.

This is your "extra energy" module. Drop this in at the end of any workday when you just need to get outside and move. Because it's a light 5K, it won't derail your Ironman recovery, and the tennis ball work keeps your fielding instincts razor-sharp without needing a practice partner or a baseball diamond.
