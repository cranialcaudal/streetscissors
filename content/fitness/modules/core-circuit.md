### Core Warm-up (4 min)
*Goal: Mobilize the spine and switch on the deep core before heavy loading.*

- [ ] **[[bosu-balance-march|Bosu balance march]] (60s)**: Stand on the dome, march in place, ribs down, core braced.
- [ ] **[[cobra-to-cat-cow|Cat–cow + thoracic rotations]] (60s)**: Mobilize the spine and ribcage.
- [ ] **[[kb-halos|Light kettlebell halos]] (60s)**: Slow circles around the head with a light bell; primes shoulders and serratus.
- [ ] **[[dead-bug|Dead bug]] (60s)**: Opposite arm/leg, low back pressed flat; switches on the deep core.
### Anti-Extension (Front Core & V-Taper)
*Goal: Resist spinal hyperextension, protecting the lower back during heavy overhead presses and the late innings of a pitch. Builds the rectus abdominis.*

- [ ] **[[bosu-hollow-hold|Bosu Hollow Hold]] (3 x 40s)**: Bosu under low back, arms overhead, legs long, low back glued down. (Builds deep core + waist tightening → V-taper). Scale by bending knees.
- [ ] **[[bosu-mountain-climbers|Bosu Mountain Climbers]] (3 x 40s)**: Hands on the dome, drive knees fast, hips low. Targets lower abs.
- [ ] **[[ab-wheel-rollouts|Ab Wheel Rollouts]] (3 x 8)**: Roll out only as far as you can maintain a neutral spine. Drop to your knees if necessary.
### Anti-Rotation & Lateral Stability (Square Waist)
*Goal: Resist twisting forces and lateral flexion. Develops the obliques as a defined block without over-widening the midsection. Crucial for transferring power from the legs to the throwing arm without leaking energy.*

- [ ] **[[bosu-plank-shoulder-taps|Bosu Plank Shoulder Taps]] (3 x 40s)**: Hands on the dome, tap opposite shoulder, hips quiet. (Anti-rotation + serratus → rib dimples). Drop to knees to scale.
- [ ] **[[kb-side-plank-reach|KB Side Plank Reach]] (3 x 40s)**: Side plank, thread the bell under the torso and back; switch sides at 20s. (Anti-lateral flexion → square waist).
- [ ] **[[pallof-press|Pallof Press]] (3 x 10/side)**: Anchor a band at chest height. Stand perpendicular to the anchor, press the band straight out, and resist the pull back.
### Rotational Power (The Pitching Engine)
*Goal: Explosive twisting and shoulder mobility. This is your pitching and hitting engine.*

- [ ] **[[kb-russian-twist|KB Russian Twist]] (3 x 40s)**: Seated, heels down or lifted, rotate from the ribs, not the arms. Target the obliques.
- [ ] **[[kb-halos|KB Halos]] (3 x 40s)**: Controlled full circles around the head, ribs down, glutes tight. (Serratus + shoulders → rib detail).
- [ ] **[[medicine-ball-slams|Medicine Ball Slams]] (3 x 10)**: Raise the ball high overhead, drop the hips, and slam it into the floor as hard as you can. Catch the bounce and repeat.
