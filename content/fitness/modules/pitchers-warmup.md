### Phase 1: Core Temperature & Footwork (15 mins)
*Goal: Break a sweat and mobilize the hips.*
- [ ] **[[outfield-jog|Outfield Jog]] (5 mins)**: Easy pace to elevate heart rate.
- [ ] **[[carioca|Carioca / Grapevine]] (3 mins)**: Fast lateral movement, focusing on deep hip rotation as the trail leg crosses over. 
- [ ] **[[high-knees-butt-kicks|High Knees & Butt Kicks]] (2 mins)**: Standard sprint prep.
- [ ] **[[fence-leg-swings|Fence Leg Swings]] (5 mins)**: Hold the fence. Forward/backward (hamstring/hip flexor) and side-to-side (abductor/adductor). 15 swings per leg, each direction.
### Phase 2: Tissue Release (15 mins)
*Goal: Use the foam roller to unstick fascia and allow joints to move through their full ranges of motion.*
- [ ] **[[thoracic-roller-extension|T-Spine Extension]] (4 mins)**: Roller perpendicular to mid-back. Support neck, extend back over roller. Restores the thoracic extension lost during the week.
- [ ] **[[lat-smashes|Lat Smashes]] (4 mins)**: Lie on your side with the roller under your armpit/lat. Roll slowly. The lats are the primary decelerators of your arm; they must be supple.
- [ ] **[[glute-it-band-roll|Glute & IT Band Roll]] (7 mins)**: Figure-four stretch on the roller for the glutes, then hit the sides of the legs. The glutes are the engine of your pitching stride.
### Phase 3: Rotational Structures & Separation (10 mins)
*Goal: Prime the kinetic chain to twist, ensuring the hips can separate from the shoulders (where pitching velocity actually comes from).*
- [ ] **[[wall-open-books|Wall Open Books]] (3 mins)**: Stand or kneel next to a wall in a split stance (inside leg forward/anchored). Reach your outside arm across your body to the wall, then open it all the way back like a book to rotate your thoracic spine while keeping your hips square. 10 reps per side.
- [ ] **[[dynamic-lunges|Dynamic Lunges with Torso Twist]] (2 mins)**: Step forward into a lunge, rotate your torso aggressively over the front leg.
- [ ] **[[90-90-hip-switches|90/90 Hip Switches]] (3 mins)**: Sit on the ground, both knees bent 90°. Without using your hands, pivot on your heels to flip your knees side to side.
- [ ] **[[quadruped-t-spine-rotations|Quadruped T-Spine Rotations]] (2 mins)**: Kneel, hand behind head, rotate elbow up to the sky.
### Phase 4: Scapular & Shoulder Band Activation (15 mins)
*Goal: Turn on the rotator cuff and scapular stabilizers using your pull-apart bands.*
- [ ] **[[band-pull-aparts|Band Pull-Aparts]] (3 mins)**: 2 x 15 reps. Strict form, squeezing shoulder blades together.
- [ ] **[[shoulder-dislocates|Shoulder Dislocates]] (3 mins)**: 2 x 10 reps. Pass the band over your head and behind your back, keeping arms straight.
- [ ] **[[fence-external-rotations|Fence-Anchored External Rotations]] (4 mins)**: Anchor band to fence. Pin elbow to ribs, rotate forearm outward. 2 x 15 per arm.
- [ ] **[[fence-internal-rotations|Fence-Anchored Internal Rotations]] (4 mins)**: Same setup, rotating inward. 2 x 15 per arm.
- [ ] **[[wall-clocks|Wall Clocks]] (1 min)**: Light, unloaded shoulder circles against a fence or wall to ensure full, painless range of motion.
### Phase 5: Forearm & Grip Prep (5 mins)
*Goal: Wake up the forearm flexors using your hand crimp. The flexors protect the UCL (Tommy John ligament) during the violent snap of a pitch.*
- [ ] **[[pulse-crimps|Pulse Crimps]] (3 mins)**: Rapid, light squeezes. The goal is to flush the forearm with blood, not to build strength.
- [ ] **[[sub-maximal-holds|Sub-Maximal Holds]] (2 mins)**: Squeeze the crimp at 50% strength and hold for 15 seconds. Repeat 3 times per hand.
