### Passive Baseball Sharpening (The Tennis Ball 5K)
*Goal: Hand-eye coordination, fielding instincts, and light aerobic flush.*

- [ ] **The 5K Run**: Maintain a relaxed, easy Zone 1/Zone 2 pace. This is not about speed; it's about active recovery and time on your feet.
- [ ] **Continuous Dribbling**: Dribble the tennis ball like a basketball as you run. This trains subconscious hand-eye coordination and peripheral vision without taxing the nervous system.
- [ ] **Wall Ball Reactive Throws**: Whenever you pass a solid brick wall or fence, throw the ball against it on the run. Field the ricochet cleanly (simulating unpredictable grounders or line drives) and keep running without breaking stride.
