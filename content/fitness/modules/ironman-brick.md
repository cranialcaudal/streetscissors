### Saturday Ironman Brick
*Goal: Race-specific endurance and training the skill of running on tired legs.*

- [ ] **The Cycle (40-60 miles)**: Steady Zone 2/3 pacing. Focus on staying aerodynamic and practicing your hydration/nutrition strategy on the bike.
- [ ] **The Transition (T2)**: Practice changing quickly from cycling shoes to running shoes to simulate race day.
- [ ] **The Run (3-10 miles)**: Your legs will feel like lead for the first mile off the bike. Focus entirely on a quick cadence and surviving that initial heaviness until your running legs kick in.
- [ ] **Folsom Simulation (Every other week)**: Drive to Folsom Lake. Simulate the actual race conditions by stacking an open-water swim right before this bike/run brick.
