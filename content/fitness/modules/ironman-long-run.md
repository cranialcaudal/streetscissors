### The Ironman Long Run
*Duration: 75+ minutes (progressing weekly).*

- [ ] **Warm-up (10 mins)**: Brisk walking, light dynamic stretching (leg swings, walking lunges).
- [ ] **Zone 2 Pacing**: The core of this run is purely aerobic. You should be able to hold a conversation. If your heart rate spikes, slow down to a walk. Do not chase pace; chase time on feet.
- [ ] **Fueling Practice**: Practice taking in your race-day nutrition (gels, hydration mix) every 30-40 minutes during the run.
