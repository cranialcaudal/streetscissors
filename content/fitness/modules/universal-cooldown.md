### Universal Decompression & Cooldown
*Goal: Restore joint mechanics, down-regulate the nervous system, and decompress the spine after any workout (swim, run, bike, or lift).*

#### Phase 1: Spine & Core Decompression
- [ ] **[[stomach-vacuum|Stomach Vacuum]] (3 x 15s)**: Exhale fully, draw the navel toward the spine, hold. Trains the TVA that cinches the waistline.
- [ ] **[[cobra-to-cat-cow|Cobra → Cat-Cow]] (45s)**: Decompress the spine and mobilize the ribcage.
- [ ] **[[childs-pose-lat-reach|Child's Pose with Lat Reach]] (45s)**: Lengthen the lats and obliques.
- [ ] **[[thoracic-roller-extension|Foam Roller Thoracic Extensions]] (2 mins)**: Roller perpendicular to mid-back. Support neck, extend back over roller.

#### Phase 2: Hips & Lower Body
- [ ] **[[couch-stretch|Couch Stretch]] (2 mins/side)**: Deep release for hip flexors and quads tightened by running, cycling, and sitting.
- [ ] **[[pigeon-pose|Pigeon Pose]] (2 mins/side)**: Opens the glutes and external rotators.
- [ ] **[[downward-dog-calf-stretch|Downward Dog Calf Stretch]] (2 mins)**: Pedal out the feet slowly to stretch the Achilles and calves.
- [ ] **[[banded-ankle-dorsiflexion|Banded Ankle Dorsiflexion]] (2 mins/side)**: Anchor a heavy band over the front ankle crease. Drive knee forward over toes.

#### Phase 3: Shoulder & Arm Care
- [ ] **[[wall-clocks|Wall Clocks]] (2 mins)**: Stand facing wall. Trace a full circle with one hand against the wall, opening the anterior capsule.
- [ ] **[[sleeper-stretch|The Sleeper Stretch]] (2 mins/side)**: Lie on side, gently push forearm toward floor with top hand. 30 sec hold, 5 sec isometric push-back.
- [ ] **[[doorway-pectoral-stretch|Doorway Pectoral Stretch]] (1 min/side)**: Open the chest after being hunched over a bike or desk.
