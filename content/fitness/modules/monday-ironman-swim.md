### Pre-Work Ironman Endurance Swim (90 Minutes)
*6:30–8:00 AM at the Davis pool, before a 9:30 start. This is your Ironman diesel engine — long, smooth, aerobic. The whole session lives in Zone 2. If you ever can't hold a conversation in your head, you're working too hard. Easy is the assignment.*

**Lap key (ARC pool):** 1 lap = down-and-back = 50 yd. Today is ~100 laps total.

> **One day after pitching.** Sunday loaded the throwing shoulder hard. This is a lot of stroke volume right behind it, so swim it *kindly*: long reach, quiet hands, no thrashing. If the shoulder is barking, shift the kick sets and backstroke earlier and cut the pull set — protect the joint, keep the aerobic work.

#### Warm-Up (15 minutes)
- [ ] **12 laps Easy Freestyle**: Find a long, relaxed rhythm. Exhale fully underwater.
- [ ] **4 × 1 lap Catch-Up Drill**: Lead hand waits at full extension until the recovering hand touches it. Builds a long, patient front-quadrant stroke — the most economical thing you can do for 2.4 miles.
- [ ] **4 × 1 lap Bilateral Breathing**: Breathe every 3rd stroke. Get comfortable on the weak side now so open-water chop never forces the issue.

#### Main Aerobic Set (55 minutes)
*Continuous and unhurried. Hold one steady easy pace the whole way through. Rest 20s between reps — just enough to reset, never enough to recover hard. This block IS the engine.*
- [ ] **3 × 16 laps Freestyle**: Long, even, controlled. Sight an imaginary buoy every 8 strokes on the middle rep.
- [ ] **4 × 4 laps Freestyle**: Same easy pace, slightly sharper focus on a clean catch.

> If the 16-lap reps feel monotonous, break the middle one as 8 laps freestyle / 8 laps pull — but keep the effort identical. The number on the clock matters less than never crossing into hard.

#### Aerobic Flush & Kick (15 minutes)
*Spare the shoulders, keep the heart rate up.*
- [ ] **6 laps Easy Kick** (board or streamline): Drive the legs, give the arms a rest.
- [ ] **6 laps Easy Backstroke**: Opens the chest and externally rotates the shoulder — the exact opposite of pitching and freestyle. Free shoulder insurance.

#### Cool-Down (5 minutes)
- [ ] **4 laps Easy Mixed**: Whatever feels good. Long exhales. Walk into the workday loose, not wrecked.
