**Where:** Baseball Diamond
**Focus:** Competing and staying healthy.

Sunday is purely about performance. Because you pitched today (or played the outfield), you must ensure that your arm is taken care of. The 60-minute pre-game warmup below is specifically engineered to protect your UCL and shoulder capsule before you even touch a baseball.

If you play tennis before the game, count it as a real shoulder cost — serves and overheads tax the same rotator cuff you're about to pitch with. On tennis days keep it light, skip max serving, and treat this warm-up as non-negotiable. A shoulder taxed before a pitching outing is exactly what shows up as the beat-up shoulder you sometimes feel by Tuesday.

After the game, go home and rest. Tomorrow is Monday—your dedicated recovery day.
