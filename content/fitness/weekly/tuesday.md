**Where:** UC Davis ARC, after the bike commute in
**Time:** ~80 min total — ~60 min necessary + ~20 min bonus aesthetic
**Bias:** Throwing arm and shoulder health. Pull volume runs ahead of press volume by design.

The bike in is your warm-up and Zone 2 cardio — the gym work is pure quality strength. The bike home is your cooldown.

**Before you touch a weight, run the Shoulder Triage at the top of Block 1.** Sunday's pitching sometimes leaves the throwing shoulder still beat up by Tuesday, and the session scales to what the cuff can actually handle that day — green, yellow, or red.

*Video links are well-regarded demos found via search; swap any that don't suit you.*

## Picking your weights

Treat every number below as a **starting point**. The real selector is reps-in-reserve: pick a load where the last rep or two of the range is genuinely hard but you could've done 1–2 more.
- Top of the range feels easy → add weight next session.
- Can't hit the bottom of the range with clean form → drop the weight.
