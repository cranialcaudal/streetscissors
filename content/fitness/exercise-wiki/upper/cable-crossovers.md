**Goal:** Isolate the chest under constant tension to drive a strong contraction and pump — accessory hypertrophy work after the main pressing.

### Execution
Set the pulleys high, take a handle in each hand, and step forward with a slight forward lean. Keeping a soft, fixed bend in the elbows, bring the hands together in front of the chest as if hugging a barrel, focusing on squeezing the chest. Control the return without letting the weight yank the arms back.

### Why it works (theory)
The rationale is constant-tension isolation: unlike a press, the cable keeps load on the chest through the whole range, including the fully-shortened position where free weights offer little resistance. That makes it a useful hypertrophy and mind-muscle finisher. It is accessory aesthetic work, not a strength or performance driver.

### Programming notes
3 x 12-15 with a focus on the squeeze, not the load. Keep the elbow angle fixed so it's the chest working, not a triceps press. Bonus block.
