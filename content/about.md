# cesar anthony moreno

I made this site using Elixir. It's just a semi-clever way of presenting markdown files. I can upload files from my phone while the machine I'm using stays plugged in at home. 

A little about the machine. I sold my GTX 1080 dream PC to family (for nothing) during the pandemic so I could get a laptop before I got to UC Davis. It was the pandemic. My major was initially Plant Science but financial pressures made me pivot into perusing an education in Cognitive Science. I do not regret the latter decision as much as I do the former. Anyway, I spent 2025 decoupling myself from Microsoft's Windows OS and dove into Linux. I don't game anymore now. I miss it.

After getting my BA in 2023, I found employment at an Alzheimer's and Dementia lab in Davis. This job has been the greatest joy of my life thus far. Here, I am able to learn about minds, brains, and computers (as Dorsey and Drayson put it (I failed the cog sci 101 class my first time lol)). It allows me to, in confidence, throw together an idea like this site with ease, given the cross application of my personal and professional skills. 

I always wanted to be a writer. This technological landscape that lays before us has reinvigorated my desire to write. The definition of the word write is beginning to slide, again. I hope that from this site, you can help me understand what it means to write again. 

The year is 2026. My goals are to ease my body of its aching, my mind of its clutter, and my soul of its weight. These are trying times. 

Expect this site to host manuscripts, audio files, and photos.
